Adopt a Shelter Cat or Dog

Our adoptable cats and dogs are ready to find their forever homes! While we recommend spaying/neutering to prevent overpopulation, it’s okay if you choose not to neuteralized your chosen pet – and for that the adoption fee will be P300 in that case. Rest assured, all our pets are thoroughly checked and free of fur bugs. Here’s how to adopt:

  1. Submit the adoption application form
  2. Attend the Zoom interview
  3. Meet our shelter animals in person
  4. Visit your chosen pet to confirm your choice
  5. Wait for vet clearance and schedule pick-up
  6. Pay the adoption fee (P300 without kapon, P500 with kapon for cats / P1000 with kapon for dogs)
  7. Take your new best friend home!
